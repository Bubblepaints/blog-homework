Delkitty

Acaras are one of many different species that exist in Neopia. As a general rule Acaras are fantastic swimmers that are just as happy on land as they are underwater. Their thick fur keeps them warm when diving to the depths of the ocean.

History

Origins

When I was a kid, Acaras were my favorite species. I just thought they looked neat, with their vaguely catlike appearance, weird ears, and horns for some reason. Delkitty got her name from the fact that Delcatty (the name of a Pok√©mon) was already taken, and Delkitty sounded close enough.

Color Choice

Yellow and pink have always been two of my favorite colors, and I felt this particular option had the best of both worlds for those color preferences.

Artwork Evolution

When Neopets redid all the artwork of the pets, Acaras got a makeover so that they could better face the camera, wear various articles of clothing, and hold items in their front paws. The faerie design became less unique in that it no longer had a different pose from other colors, but kept the faerie wings.

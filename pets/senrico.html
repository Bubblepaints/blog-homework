Senrico

Shoyrus are one of many different species that exist in Neopia. Shoyrus are fantastic flyers who love to soar through the skies. Contrary to popular belief, Shoyrus do not breathe fire!

History

Origins

Shoyrus are the number one most popular Neopet, by the website's own statistics, meaning that more people have created a Shoyru than any other species. Statistics aside, I've always liked dragons as well, and so I thought a cool-looking pirate dragon would be a fun pet to have.

Color Choice

There are many good color options for Shoyrus, and Senrico has gone through several in his lifetime. Originally, I created him with the color Blue. I then painted him rainbow, which he stayed as for several years, before finally deciding on Pirate. In the old days, before Neopets could wear clothing, the colors that added clothing were highly coveted.

Artwork Evolution

When Neopets redid all the artwork of the pets, Shoyrus got a makeover so that they could better face the camera, wear various articles of clothing, and hold items in their front paws. Regrettably, this meant that a lot of the Neopets that had fun and unique poses lost those poses, including the Pirate Shoyru. I think the new design is fine, but I do still like the look of the old one better. My favorite addition is the piercing on the tail fin.

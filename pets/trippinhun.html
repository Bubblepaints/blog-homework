trippinhun

Cybunnies are one of many different species that exist in Neopia. As a general rule Cybunnies love the colder regions of Neopia, their thick white fur not only keeps them warm, but also helps them blend in with the snowy mountainside.

History

Origins

Going along with what should now be an obvious theme, I just thought bunnies were very cute, and got myself a bunny-like pet. Technically, I was trying to obtain a different baby pet through less than legal means (the old "send me 100,000 Neopoints and I'll send you my rare pet" scam), but the scammer realized she had little time left with her account, and just said "here, take this one, I don't care anymore."

Color Choice

Trippinhun came pre-painted, which is great because in those days, a Baby Paint Brush was very expensive. The design wasn't the greatest, but the idea was there, so I used the excuse of her color to draw a baby bunny and enter it in the drawing competition.

Artwork Evolution

When Neopets redid all the artwork of the pets, Cybunnies got a makeover so that they could better face the camera, wear various articles of clothing, and hold items in their front paws. Baby pets remain difficult to customize, as they're still just a little too custom to really do anything with. My favorite part of this change is that they now look less cross-eyed.
